Hello, I am Jasmine Griffiths, a sound designer working in Naarm.

I am drawn to work that has something to say, and am particularly enamoured with nature and storytelling - and this informs my naturalistic approach to sound design. I'm always looking for new ways to explore the sonic medium, and push the boundaries of my expertise.

I specialise in detailed, realistic and creative sound design - my aim is always to create soundscapes you can get lost in and forget your reality.

I also have experience with dialogue editing, audio restoration, field recording and more.

I am currently completing my Bachelor of Design (Digital Media) at RMIT in Naarm. I have also done volunteer audio work for community radio (SYN Media) and community television (RMITV).

If you'd like to collaborate on a project, don't hesitate to get in touch!
