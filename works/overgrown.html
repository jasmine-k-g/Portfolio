Overgrown
Sound, music and words by Jasmine Griffiths
Voice of the protagonist by Sophie Harvey
Voice of the friend by Cleo Fawcett

Overgrown is a work of narrative fiction following a young woman who is living alone for the first time, and whose houseplants do not make very good company.

This is a binaural audio work, it is best experienced with headphones.
